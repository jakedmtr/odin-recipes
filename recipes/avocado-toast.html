Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is delicious as a simple breakfast, snack or light meal!

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Sourdough bread
  • Lemon
  • Chili
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the avocado in half and carefully remove its stone, then scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Squeeze in the lemon juice then mash with a fork to your desired texture. Season to taste with sea salt, black pepper and chilli flakes.
  3. Toast your bread, drizzle over the oil then pile the avocado on top.
