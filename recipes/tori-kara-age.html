Tori Kara Age

Tori kara age is chicken breasts that have been cut into finger size pieces, breaded, and then deep fried. The result is a deliciously addictive, crunchy and juicy piece of chicken that will satisfy anyone's taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sake
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • MSG
  • Potato starch
  • Lemon
  • Rapeseed oil

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces each about the size of half a lime.
  2. Put all the cut chicken into a ziplock bag and add the soy sauce, mirin, sake, pepper, paprika, and MSG. Mix thoroughly for a few minutes. Leave in the fridge for around 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a small pan with rapeseed oil on a low-medium heat.
  4. Take out the chicken from the fridge and empty all the contents from the ziplock bag, draining all the liquid. Cover each piece of chicken completely in potato starch.
  5. Fry the chicken in the pan for about 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Put on a plate with a paper towel to absorb the oil.
  6. Move to a plate and garnish with a lemon wedge.
